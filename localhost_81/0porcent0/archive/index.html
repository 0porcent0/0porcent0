What is the importance of the sharing role in the creative process?

  • TALK

19.03.2022 Tags: Espinho | PT

As in the starting point, your way throught building a project is not necessarily defined or clear. The artwork goes through a process that is not always linear between the starting and the finalizing point. In a personal perspective, we feel the need to pause working at some point and expose it to the vulnerability of sharing with others. Vulnerability because in that stage, the project is still a prototype and sharing means to understand if its going to be recieve as we mean it to be, as the porpuse we as artists supposedly gave it. Sharing at this stage of the process is a crucial moment, moving away from the project so it recieves new and others perceptions.

What is the role of the spectator in the art creation?

  • TALK

30.04.2021 Tags: Porto | PT

As soon as shared, the artwork is immediately delivered to the spectator’s choice. The author loses the control upon the contemplation, interpretation, interaction or appropriation process. But the artwork lives, exactly, at each new interpretation, it becomes almost a living being, mutant ( so the loss between the artwork and its representation in an image, reduces divergence by being conditioned  by the photography riddle. The artwork reproduction in a photograph format, is halfway of the spectator route in the reception of the artwork.

Practically, there exists a linearity in the relation of creator-artwork-spectator. Even though an artwork can be seized by a vast public, by every public that we can imagine, doesn’t it carry, the piece itself, the figure that the spectator aims to constitute? In this line of thought, the spectator surge before the artwork is shared, it is, as in the design practice, the target audience. Beyond the artwork, the creator becomes a spectator creator? Maybe the artwork is a search for a spectator that fits, and the creator’s position takes the final place as gage between the relation of the piece and spectator.

Even not knowing how this text is going to be received, the truth is that there exists a small gap of possibilities where the reader can dovetail, and by that, the bigger gap where it can not dovetail. It can be recieve by both, but it wasn’t part of the creation and naturally, part of the artwork.
